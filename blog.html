Analysis of Mr Narendra Modi's Speeches

The speeches by prominent leaders are often a reflection of the current Government's vision, plans and progress. This work is about analyzing the speeches of incumbent Prime Minister of India, Mr Narendra Modi. Although, Mr. Modi's speeches are individually analyzed by media houses and political pundits, an aggregated overview is also important. To answer questions like - how frequent are the speeches? How do the national and international issues addressed evolve over time? What are the factors that make a speech popular?
