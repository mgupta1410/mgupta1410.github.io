Research Interests

I am interested in the areas of Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing with the focus on addressing global social problems. My work has involved behavioral analysis and influence propagation on social network, recommendation systems and information retrieval on large corpora. I often wonder about ethical biases that creep in ML algorithms and how to make them more fair and interpretable.

Spam and Relevance

I currently solve research problems at LinkedIn as a part of their Spam and Relevance team at Bangalore, headed by Dr. Rushi Bhatt. As a team, our objective is to throttle generation and spread of spam, unprofessional and low quality content on LinkedIn. We build Machine Learning based models to classify spam, predict virality and improve feed quality. I am currently working on a super interesting project aiming at quantifying tendency of LinkedIn members to spread low quality content on LinkedIn network.

Search

For more than a year, I worked in Information Retrieval domain at LinkedIn in their Search team. We used LinkedIn's search infrastructure, Galene, to build search on LinkedIn which millions of people use every day. I worked to build autosuggest and regular search on companies, universities, articles, Slideshare presentations and Lynda courses. I also pursued my undergraduate thesis in the same team where I worked on detecting near duplicate articles and demoting the duplicate elements in search.
